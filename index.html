Orca Whale

Despite their common name, Killer Whales are actually the largest members of the dolphin family.

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Average Length: 16-26 ft
  • Average Lifespan: Females up to 90 years, Males:50-60 years.
  • Habitat: Orcas have a global distribution & are found in all of the world's oceans

Killer Whales (Orcinus orca), are highly intelligent & social, living in close-knit pods with unique cultures. They use sophisticated hunting techniques & display strong family bonds, playful behaviors, & advanced problem-solving skills.
